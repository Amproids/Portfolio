Quick Summary

My name is Andrew Parry, I'm a programmer from Utah. I'm hoping to be a game dev. I always found programming amazing and I want to make stuff that other people can enjoy. Starting with small stuff on scratch and working my way up to my own systems and more ambitious projects. I hope you find this website entertaining and that you stay a while!

Growing Up

I grew up in Utah, nice place. The air is a little thin and it's dry, so the weather is pretty all over the place. Really cold winters, really hot summers. But it was a good place to grow up. Lots of variety and lots of opportunity to learn.

I went on a lot of camping trips and played a lot outside growing up. When I was younger and first learned about video games that was pretty cool. I first played on the PlayStation2. Mafia 2 and Crash Badicoot 3 were big ones I remember. Later on I learned about flash games on sites like Armor Games, Game Sloth, and Coolmathgames. And even later on, I got into PC games like Warcraft 3, Halo 1, and Lego Chess. (that was a fun one.)
